Pandemic

Pandemic!! Stop the the virus from infecting the the United States of America

Every round you get 2 actions you can take, as designated by the Action Counter. You can drive to a close city, fly to a city you have a card for and research if you are in a city that has a research station. After 2 actions a new round starts and a new city gets infected. Everytime a city gets infected, it receives 4 cubes. You can cure via the "Cure Button" to remove 1 cube at the cost of one action point. If you have researched the appropriate color in a research station you can remove all disease cubes from an infected city if it matches the researched color.

If you are in a city with a research station, you have access to a research button. By hitting the button, you can research the disease by spending a city card. Your research progress will go towards the corresponding city color. You will find the cure after attaining 4 research points.

If 5 cities are infected with 4 disease cubes, you lose the game. If there are 8 cured cities simultainiously, you win the game.
